Welcome to FLOOM!

At Floom we’re proud to be bringing the local florist experience online, reminding people

just how special it is to both send flowers and have flowers delivered to you.

We aim to combine a beautiful to look at, easy-to-use and flexible flower delivery service

with the spark and magic that only the most passionate and skilled local florists can bring to a bouquet.

We handpick every florist that we work with to ensure they share our values and our

commitment to providing amazing online flower delivery experiences.

When you order flowers online, we don’t believe you should simply be a last-minute gift choice

(though our same-day flower delivery option does help with that!). Flowers at their best can truly excite

the senses, whatever the occasion, whatever the location: from Mother’s Day flowers in Bristol to Valentine’s Day flowers in London.

Every item that you see on our site is utterly unique to the local florist who crafts it, which means

it will also be unique to the sender and loved one who receives it. We don’t believe in providing our florists with

generic guidelines, instead, we let them create bouquets based on nature’s seasonality and their own creative instincts.
